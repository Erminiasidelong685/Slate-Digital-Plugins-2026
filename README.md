## 💡 Usage Tips

Place your EQ plugins early in your signal chain to shape the tone before applying dynamics. Use the compressor on individual tracks to smooth out spikes in volume. Apply the limiter at the end of your master channel to lock in your volume levels. Monitor your audio carefully to ensure clean processing. If you experience high CPU usage, bounce your tracks to audio files to free up processing power.
